about Business

OUR COMPANY

NARPAVI Engineering & Consultancy is a well-established and highly respected firm that has been operating since 2021 with its office located in India. Our expertise covers a wide range of specialized areas such as Engineering Consultancy, design of Residential, Commercial, Industrial Developments, Institutional, Hotel, Leisure Facilities, Oil & Gas, Road and Infrastructure, Environmental Solutions, BIM services, and more. At NARPAVI, we are proud of our unique and creative approach to designing innovative projects that perfectly meet the diverse needs of our clients. We are committed to delivering exceptional results, which has earned us an impressive list of clients, including leading contractors and individuals. We have successfully executed projects of all sizes and complexities, from leisure facilities to industrial buildings and plants. Our designs are renowned for their unparalleled concept, execution, and responsiveness to our clients' needs.
